This is the open-source quoFEM application.

The Quantified Uncertainty with Optimization for the Finite Element Method (quoFEM) is an open-source research application which focuses on providing uncertainty quantification methods (forward, inverse, reliability, sensitivity and parameter estimation) to researchers in natural hazards who utilize existing simulation software applications, typically Finite Element applications, in their work.
